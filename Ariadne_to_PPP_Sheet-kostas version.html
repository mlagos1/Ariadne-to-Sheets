How it works
  1. In Ariadne, expand Advanced Options so every category is visible, then copy the whole Algo box and paste it into box 1. It reads the two score rate values (Home / Away SR) and the Supremacy/Expectancy list, ignoring everything else.
  2. One paste: click Copy, click cell B6 on the PPP leagues sheet, paste. The whole block (B6:C11) lands at once — row 7 is re-written with the same Supremacy / Expectancy headers, so nothing is lost.
  3. Two pastes: if you'd rather not touch row 7, switch the toggle — one block goes to B6, the other to B8.
  4. Missing data is left blank (flagged amber in the preview) rather than guessed.
